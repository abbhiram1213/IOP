Farming Questions Answered

We have put together this Frequently Asked Questions section to help buyers and farmers quickly find answers to common questions about using our online crop marketplace.
How do I list my crops for sale?

Go to the Sell tab, select Add New Listing, choose the type of crop, fill in details like variety, quantity available, price, etc. Upload photos too. That's it!

What crops can I buy on this platform?

A wide variety - grains like wheat and rice as well as vegetables, fruits, spices, pulses, cotton, coffee, tea and more. Browse or search to find the crops you need.

How do I pay farmers for crops purchased?

We offer secure online payment options as well as bank transfers. Choose what works for you during checkout.

Are there shipping options for crops bought online?

Yes, we partner with logistics companies to offer affordable shipping rates across India. We'll connect you to arrange pickup and delivery.
